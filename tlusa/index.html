STATS GLOSSARY
General
90s
Number of 90-minute games worth of minutes played. 1.0 = one full match.
G+
Goals Added — ASA's all-in-one value metric measuring how much a player increases their team's chance of scoring vs. conceding.
ATK EFF
Attacking Efficiency — Touchline USA composite score (0–100) weighting goals, xG, shots on target, assists, xAG, key passes, and Goals Added.
DEF EFF
Defensive Efficiency — Touchline USA composite score (0–100) using Goals Added and pass completion as defensive proxies.
Attacking Stats (per 90 min)
GLS/90
Goals scored per 90 minutes.
xG/90
Expected Goals per 90 — the quality of shots taken based on location, angle, and assist type. Higher = better chances.
SHOT AST
Shot Assists — passes that directly created a shot attempt leading to a goal, per ASA's model. May differ from official MLS assist totals.
xAG/90
Expected Assisted Goals per 90 — expected goals generated by a player's key passes, regardless of whether the shot scored.
SoT/90
Shots on Target per 90 — shots that would have gone in if not saved.
KP/90
Key Passes per 90 — passes that created a shot attempt.
Goals Added Breakdown
SHOOTING
G+ from shot quality and placement — above or below what's expected given shot location.
PASSING
G+ from pass quality — completing harder passes and creating dangerous opportunities.
DRIBBLING
G+ from carrying the ball — advancing play and beating defenders.
RECEIVING
G+ from how a player receives the ball — positioning and touch to retain possession in dangerous areas.
FOULING
G+ from foul behavior — drawing fouls in dangerous areas adds value; committing them subtracts it.
INTERRUPT
G+ from interrupting the opponent — tackles, interceptions, and blocks that disrupt attacks.
Goalkeeper Stats
GK EFF
GK Efficiency — Touchline USA composite score (0–100) combining Save%, GA/90, PSxG-GA, and Goals Added.
SAVE%
Percentage of shots on target that were saved.
GA/90
Goals Allowed per 90 minutes. Lower is better.
PSxG-GA
Post-Shot Expected Goals minus Goals Allowed — measures how many goals a keeper prevented beyond what was expected. Positive = above average.
G+
Goals Added above replacement for goalkeepers, accounting for shot-stopping, sweeping, passing, and claiming.
Salary & Value
SALARY
Guaranteed Compensation — base salary plus annualized signing bonuses. Published by the MLS Players Association (typically released in spring).
g+/$M
Goals Added per $1 million of guaranteed compensation — measures how much on-field value a player delivers per dollar spent. Higher = better value.
Team Stats
GP
Games Played.
GF / GA
Goals For / Goals Against — actual goals scored and conceded.
GD
Goal Difference — GF minus GA. Positive = scoring more than conceding.
xGF / xGA
Expected Goals For / Against — sum of shot quality created or allowed. More predictive of future results than actual goals.
xGD
Expected Goal Difference — xGF minus xGA. Positive = creating better chances than allowed.
GD-xGD
Actual GD minus Expected GD — measures luck or finishing quality. Positive = outperforming expected output.
SF / SA
Shots For / Against — total shot attempts created and allowed.
PTS
Points — standard league points (W=3, D=1, L=0).
xPTS
Expected Points — points a team would expect to earn based on xG in each game. Smooths out variance from actual results.
TEAM EFF
Team Efficiency — composite 0-100 score based on xGF, xGA, xGD, and xPoints.
Team Passing Style
PASS COMP %
Pass Completion Percentage — share of attempted passes successfully completed.
xPASS COMP %
Expected Pass Completion % — predicted completion rate given the difficulty of passes attempted.
PCOE/100
Passes Completed Over Expected per 100 — how many more (or fewer) passes a team completes than expected. Positive = better than expected.
AVG VERT DIST
Average Vertical Distance — avg yards gained forward per pass. Higher = more direct, progressive style.
OPP PASS COMP %
Opponent Pass Completion % — how well opponents pass against this team. Lower = better defensive pressure.
PCOE DIFF
Passes Completed Over Expected Difference — team PCOE minus opponent PCOE. Positive = team passes better relative to their opponents.
Team G+ Action Breakdown
Shooting
G+ from shot-taking — value from the quality and placement of shots. High For = efficient attackers.
Passing
G+ from passing — value created by moving the ball into dangerous areas. High For = strong build-up play.
Receiving
G+ from receiving — value from getting into position to receive dangerous passes. Reflects off-ball movement.
Dribbling
G+ from dribbling — value from carrying the ball past defenders into threatening areas.
Interrupting
G+ from interceptions/blocks — value from winning the ball or disrupting attacks. High For = pressing, defensive team.
Fouling
G+ from fouling — value from winning or conceding fouls in dangerous areas.
