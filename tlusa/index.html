STATS GLOSSARY
General
90s
Number of 90-minute games worth of minutes played. 1.0 = one full match.
G+
Goals Added ‚Äî ASA's all-in-one value metric measuring how much a player increases their team's chance of scoring vs. conceding.
ATK EFF
Attacking Efficiency ‚Äî Touchline USA composite score (0‚Äì100) weighting goals, xG, shots on target, assists, xAG, key passes, and Goals Added.
DEF EFF
Defensive Efficiency ‚Äî Touchline USA composite score (0‚Äì100) using Goals Added and pass completion as defensive proxies.
Attacking Stats (per 90 min)
GLS/90
Goals scored per 90 minutes.
xG/90
Expected Goals per 90 ‚Äî the quality of shots taken based on location, angle, and assist type. Higher = better chances.
SHOT AST
Shot Assists ‚Äî passes that directly created a shot attempt leading to a goal, per ASA's model. May differ from official MLS assist totals.
xAG/90
Expected Assisted Goals per 90 ‚Äî expected goals generated by a player's key passes, regardless of whether the shot scored.
SoT/90
Shots on Target per 90 ‚Äî shots that would have gone in if not saved.
KP/90
Key Passes per 90 ‚Äî passes that created a shot attempt.
Goals Added Breakdown
SHOOTING
G+ from shot quality and placement ‚Äî above or below what's expected given shot location.
PASSING
G+ from pass quality ‚Äî completing harder passes and creating dangerous opportunities.
DRIBBLING
G+ from carrying the ball ‚Äî advancing play and beating defenders.
RECEIVING
G+ from how a player receives the ball ‚Äî positioning and touch to retain possession in dangerous areas.
FOULING
G+ from foul behavior ‚Äî drawing fouls in dangerous areas adds value; committing them subtracts it.
INTERRUPT
G+ from interrupting the opponent ‚Äî tackles, interceptions, and blocks that disrupt attacks.
Goalkeeper Stats
GK EFF
GK Efficiency ‚Äî Touchline USA composite score (0‚Äì100) combining Save%, GA/90, PSxG-GA, and Goals Added.
SAVE%
Percentage of shots on target that were saved.
GA/90
Goals Allowed per 90 minutes. Lower is better.
PSxG-GA
Post-Shot Expected Goals minus Goals Allowed ‚Äî measures how many goals a keeper prevented beyond what was expected. Positive = above average.
G+
Goals Added above replacement for goalkeepers, accounting for shot-stopping, sweeping, passing, and claiming.
Salary & Value
SALARY
Guaranteed Compensation ‚Äî base salary plus annualized signing bonuses. Published by the MLS Players Association (typically released in spring).
g+/$M
Goals Added per $1 million of guaranteed compensation ‚Äî measures how much on-field value a player delivers per dollar spent. Higher = better value.
Team Stats
GP
Games Played.
GF / GA
Goals For / Goals Against ‚Äî actual goals scored and conceded.
GD
Goal Difference ‚Äî GF minus GA. Positive = scoring more than conceding.
xGF / xGA
Expected Goals For / Against ‚Äî sum of shot quality created or allowed. More predictive of future results than actual goals.
xGD
Expected Goal Difference ‚Äî xGF minus xGA. Positive = creating better chances than allowed.
GD-xGD
Actual GD minus Expected GD ‚Äî measures luck or finishing quality. Positive = outperforming expected output.
SF / SA
Shots For / Against ‚Äî total shot attempts created and allowed.
PTS
Points ‚Äî standard league points (W=3, D=1, L=0).
xPTS
Expected Points ‚Äî points a team would expect to earn based on xG in each game. Smooths out variance from actual results.
TEAM EFF
Team Efficiency ‚Äî composite 0-100 score based on xGF, xGA, xGD, and xPoints.
Team Passing Style
PASS COMP %
Pass Completion Percentage ‚Äî share of attempted passes successfully completed.
xPASS COMP %
Expected Pass Completion % ‚Äî predicted completion rate given the difficulty of passes attempted.
PCOE/100
Passes Completed Over Expected per 100 ‚Äî how many more (or fewer) passes a team completes than expected. Positive = better than expected.
AVG VERT DIST
Average Vertical Distance ‚Äî avg yards gained forward per pass. Higher = more direct, progressive style.
OPP PASS COMP %
Opponent Pass Completion % ‚Äî how well opponents pass against this team. Lower = better defensive pressure.
PCOE DIFF
Passes Completed Over Expected Difference ‚Äî team PCOE minus opponent PCOE. Positive = team passes better relative to their opponents.
Team G+ Action Breakdown
Shooting
G+ from shot-taking ‚Äî value from the quality and placement of shots. High For = efficient attackers.
Passing
G+ from passing ‚Äî value created by moving the ball into dangerous areas. High For = strong build-up play.
Receiving
G+ from receiving ‚Äî value from getting into position to receive dangerous passes. Reflects off-ball movement.
Dribbling
G+ from dribbling ‚Äî value from carrying the ball past defenders into threatening areas.
Interrupting
G+ from interceptions/blocks ‚Äî value from winning the ball or disrupting attacks. High For = pressing, defensive team.
Fouling
G+ from fouling ‚Äî value from winning or conceding fouls in dangerous areas.
